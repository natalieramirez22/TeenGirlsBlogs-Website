Teen Girls Blogs

Hello! My name is Natalie Ramirez and my sister Erika and I created this website for teen girls (like ourselves) who are bored but want to be productive and/or just do something that's not going to waste time and be boring.

With this website, you can get inspiration to do so many things and also ideas of what to do. Our mission is to get smart teen girls out of bed and stop watching so much Netflix and do something cool and interesting. Of course, I love Netflix too and love watching TV and movies, but we have to do more than that, especially now that we are at home all day with nothing to do.

Anyway, summer is about to begin! So you know what that means... a lot of sitting around and doing nothing. Yeah, quarantine sucks, but hopefully Erika and I can provide you with lots of ideas for you to either get ahead in school, make a cool new project, find your interests, give you good movie, show, or book recommendations, or advice for your social life and more!
